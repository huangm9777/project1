The most important festival in a year -- Spring Festival

The Spring Festival, also known as the lunar New Year, is the beginning of the year in the traditional sense of the festival. The Spring Festival has a long history, it evolved from the ancient times. The origin of the Spring Festival contains profound cultural connotation, in the inheritance and development of the rich historical and cultural deposits. During the Spring Festival, all over the country held a variety of celebrations, with strong regional characteristics, vivid and festive atmosphere is full of; These activities, which mainly delete the old and the new, promote evil spirits and prevent disasters, worship gods and ancestors, pray for the New Year, are colorful in form and embody the essence of traditional Chinese culture.

Traditional folk

The traditional festival ceremony and related custom activities are the important content of the festival elements, bearing the rich and colorful festival culture.Year festival is an ancient festival in China, is also the most important festival of the whole year, in the historical development, mixed with a variety of local folk customs as a whole, formed some relatively fixed customs and habits, many are still handed down to the present.During the Spring Festival, the celebration activities are extremely rich and varied, such as lion dance, floating color, dragon dance, goddess tour, temple fair, flower market, flower lantern tour, percussion tour, vernier flag, fireworks, blessing, spring, also walking on stilts, land boating, yangko dance and so on.The custom of offering sacrifices to the gods (ancestors) prevails in the coastal areas of south China, inheriting ancient customs. During the Spring Festival, many places hold grand and grand activities such as offering sacrifices to the gods of heaven and earth, greeting jubilee and praying for blessings, etc.

The Spring Festival is a festival of joy and harmony, family reunion, is also a carnival for people to express their desire for happiness and freedom and the spiritual pillar forever.Before the festival, people who have left their hometown try their best to get back home and reunite with their families.On New Year's eve, the whole family gathered together to eat the "reunion dinner", elders to the children distributed "lucky money", and then "shousui", to welcome the New Year.During the festival, friends and relatives visit each other to express their feelings and good wishes for the New Year.

The traditional customs

To stick couplets

Every family "paste red" (red is the Spring Festival couplets, door gods, hengbi, New Year pictures, "fu" word, and other New Year paste red festive elements collectively).The New Year paste red (spring), is a traditional Chinese New Year customs, adds a festive atmosphere, and placed people to the New Year and a new life of good expectations.

Family reunion dinner

New Year's eve dinner, also known as the year's dinner, reunion dinner, reunion dinner, especially refers to the New Year's eve of the family dinner.New Year's eve dinner originated from the ancient end-of-the-year sacrificial ceremony, worship the gods and ancestors after the reunion dinner.New Year's eve dinner is the highlight of the year before, not only rich and colorful, and very pay attention to the head.Eat the reunion dinner before the worship of god ancestor, worship ceremony after the completion of the meal.There are generally chicken on the table (meaning has plan), fish (meaning every year), oyster sauce (meaning good city), vegetable (meaning rich), bean curd (meaning rich), lotus root (meaning smart), lettuce (meaning wealth), raw garlic (meaning will calculate), sausage (meaning for a long time) in order to auspicious.Chinese New Year's eve dinner is a family reunion dinner, which is the most sumptuous and important dinner at the end of the year.

New Year's money

New Year's money, one of the customs, after dinner the elders will be prepared in advance of the New Year's money sent to the younger generation, it is said that the New Year's money can suppress evil, the younger generation can get the New Year's money to spend a year in peace.Lucky money in folk culture means to ward off evil spirits and ward off ghosts, bless peace.The money was originally intended to ward off evil spirits.Because people think that children are vulnerable to sneaky attacks, so use lucky money to suppress evil spirits.
